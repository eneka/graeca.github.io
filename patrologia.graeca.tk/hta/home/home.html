Welcome to patrologia.graeca.tk


This is a simple tool in order to search for specific combination of words in Patrologia Graeca.
The search is done in atonic Greek and the results are returned in atonic Greek.
The search needs at least three greek characters and is done for all the writings of a specific author.
The returned text has the line number of the findings and the specific book which was found.
You can check for more details to the complete text of the author on the right list.
Remember to have set unicode encoding to your browser, also enable javascript.
